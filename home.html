About

GStress is a mobile website targeted towards students at Gettysburg College and the ongoing feeling of stress that they feel. Through this website students are able to access a platform that provides them with a variety of resources. Resources that range from tips, setting and tracking personal goals with their unique profile, and resources on campus.
